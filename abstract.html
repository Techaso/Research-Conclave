Call for Abstract submission

All those desirous of participating and/or presenting a poster in the conclave are requested to complete the online abstract submission process. Authors are requested to submit a one page abstract in the prescribed format mentioned in website. Abstracts for poster presentation should be in Times New Roman with in A4 format. The title should be in bold with font size of 14 followed by author(s) name in next line and affiliation in subsequent line(s). This should follow the email ID of the presenting author. The template may be downloaded from HERE. The deadline for abstract submission has been extended to 5th April 2018. Participants would be intimated by email about the acceptance of the abstract by 8th April 2018. Registration process would initiate on 8th April 2018.        

SUBMIT YOUR ABSTRACT AT "research.conclave@iitrpr.ac.in".
