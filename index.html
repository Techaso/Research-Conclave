RESEARCH CONCLAVE

|




Last date for Abstract Submission has been extended to 5th April 2018. Notification for Abstract Acceptance is 8th April 2018. Registration starts on 8th April 2018.

Research conclave 2018 is being organized by Indian Institute of Technology Ropar which aims to provide a potential platform to nurture the young researchers and students from the region to keep pace with the latest in the field. The conclave is not just to boost the energy and enthusiasm of researchers but also to recognize the efforts of all. It proposes to host several luminaries from the academia as speakers. It works as a catalyst in readdressing the academic research challenges and putting forth tremendous efforts in the direction of betterment of society. The aim of this conclave is to bring together scientists and scholars on a common platform to share and exchange new ideas and application experiences face to face, to establish research relations and find collaborators for future work. It will be focusing on several topics in the field of science, engineering, humanities and social science, bringing out cutting-edge developments in various fields. It works as a bridge, connecting various fields opening the gates for interdisciplinary research. This research conclave is a debut event which is expected to be a milestone for the coming years. On behalf of the organizing committee we would like to invite you to participate in the conclave.

Research @ IIT Ropar

IIT Ropar has made serious efforts to align its R&D focus with the intent of making a positive impact on the world. We attract some of the best students and faculty that conduct research projects in advanced areas of science and engineering. The Institute has ongoing academic and research collaborations with many national and international universities, in order to keep pace with expanding frontiers of knowledge.

IIT Ropar has reached a great mile stone in July 2017 by crossing 1000 publications mark in the Scopus database which is the world's largest abstract and citation database of peer-reviewed research publications. As on date number of our publications indexed in Scopus is 1020 with an average citations per paper of 9.33 on March 2018 which is highest among eight newer IITs.
